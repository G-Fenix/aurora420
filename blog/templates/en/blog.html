FAQ: Cannabis and Association Cannabis Clubs

Is cannabis addictive?

It can be psychologically habit-forming, especially in heavy long-term use, but it's not physically addictive like nicotine.

What's the difference between hemp and cannabis?

Hemp has low THC (under 0.3%) and is used industrially. Cannabis is bred for THC-rich flowers used for consumption.

How long does a cannabis high last?

It depends on the method. Smoking: 1–3 hours. Edibles: up to 6–8 hours. It also varies by dosage and individual tolerance.

Are edibles stronger than smoking?

Yes. Edibles are metabolized differently in the liver, making the effects more intense and longer-lasting than smoking or vaping.

Can I drive after using cannabis?

No. Driving under the influence of cannabis is illegal and dangerous. Reaction time and judgment are impaired.

Can I grow my own cannabis?

Depends on your country or state. Some places allow home cultivation for medical or personal use; others prohibit it completely.

Is cannabis safe for teenagers?

Cannabis use during adolescence may impact brain development and mental health. It is not recommended for minors.

What's the difference between CBD and THC?

THC causes a high and psychoactive effects. CBD is non-psychoactive and commonly used for relaxation, pain relief, and anxiety.

Is cannabis legal in Spain?

Cannabis is decriminalized for personal use in private spaces. It’s illegal to consume or possess it publicly.

What are cannabis clubs?

Cannabis clubs are private associations where registered members can grow and consume cannabis collectively.

Do I need ID to enter a cannabis club?

Yes, you must be over 18 or 21 (depending on the region) and show valid ID to join a club.

Can tourists join cannabis clubs?

Some clubs allow tourists with a valid ID and local sponsor. However, this is a legal grey area.

Is cannabis dangerous for pets?

Yes, cannabis is toxic to dogs and cats. Keep edibles and flowers far away from pets at all times.

Does weed affect creativity?

Many users report enhanced creativity and imagination, especially with sativa strains. However, results vary by person.

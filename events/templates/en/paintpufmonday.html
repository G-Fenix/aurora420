🎨 What is Paint & Puff Monday?

A relaxed and creative competition where you paint, puff, and let your imagination flow!

🎯 How It Works

Every participant receives the **same reference image**. You’ll have **15 minutes** to paint it using the materials we provide. Just bring your vibe — we’ve got the rest!

📜 The Rules

  • 🖼 Everyone paints the same image.
  • ⏱ You have 15 minutes to complete your art.
  • 🗳 All players vote anonymously.
  • 🙊 You won’t know whose art is whose!
  • 🏆 The one with the most votes wins a free joint!

🕒 Game Time

The game starts at 18:00 every Monday in the lounge zone. Arrive a bit early to grab your spot and supplies — space is limited!
